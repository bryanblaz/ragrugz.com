The Founder

Bryan Anderson founded RagRugz™ in 2022. After discovering the world of tufting,

it unveiled a canvas where imagination and craftsmanship intertwine, inviting one

to weave their unique story into every loop and pile, and open a doorway to a realm

where fibers transform into art. A vibrant journey of creativity, where every knot

and stitch tells a unique story. The joy of weaving colors and patterns together

fills the heart with excitement, turning threads into a tapestry of beauty. With

each completed rug, a sense of fulfillment and pride blossoms, celebrating the

artistry and craftsmanship behind every piece. This rug company transcends the

ordinary by weaving its creativity into more than just rugs. From bespoke clothing

that carries the warmth of home to intricate art pieces that tell stories of craftsmanship,

their offerings extend far beyond the floor. Embracing novelty, they reimagine everyday

items, infusing them with the cozy charm and timeless elegance of their signature rugs.
