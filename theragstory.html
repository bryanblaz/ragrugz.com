The Rag Story

Ragrugz™ was more than just a design decision—it was an expression of the essence of the company, a visual representation of its values, creativity, and vision. The colors and shapes that make up the Ragrugz logo are a tapestry of meaning, woven together to tell a story of inspiration, innovation, and community.

The Colors of Creativity and Connection

Blue: Trust, wisdom, stability; representing creativity and reliability.

Yellow: Happiness, energy, optimism; symbolizing joy and innovation.

Green: Growth, harmony, and freshness; highlighting sustainability and evolution.

Red: Passion, energy, action; embodying dedication and boldness.

The Shapes of Unity and Innovation

Circles: Represent unity, wholeness, and inclusion, symbolizing endless creativity.

The Essence of Ragrugz

Creativity: Blue and yellow represent innovation and joy.

Community: Green symbolizes sustainable practices and connection to nature.

Passion: Red embodies the drive and enthusiasm of our brand.

Balance and Harmony: The interplay of colors and shapes reflects the harmony in our designs.
